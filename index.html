The Man Behind the Machine

Meet Jim

Jim & Family in South Florida 2025
Jim McCowien
Founder, Owner & Lead Operator

Jim McCowien built this company from the ground up, literally. With over 20 years of hands-on experience in underground construction, he started McCowien Construction LLC in 2003 with a simple belief: do the work right, treat people fairly, and never cut corners.

A graduate of Reynolds High School, Jim resides in Greenville, Pennsylvania. When he's not on a job site, you'll find him and his family at the farm house, cheering at sporting events, or soaking up the sun on the beach. When you hire McCowien Construction, you're working directly with the owner, not a middleman.
